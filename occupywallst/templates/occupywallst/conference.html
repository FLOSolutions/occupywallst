Information

A conference bridge (or party line) is a phone number several or more people can dial to have a meeting by phone. This is useful if you don't want to reveal your phone number or want to chat with more than three people (because then you could just have a three-way call.)

To get started, ask your group to call into one of the numbers below at a specified time. Please also be aware that anyone is able to listen to your conversation.

Once a conference has 2+ members, an "MP3 Stream" link will appear to allow others to listen to the meeting. Please note that when streaming there's a 30 second audio delay.
