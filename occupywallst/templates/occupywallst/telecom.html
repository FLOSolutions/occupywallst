Information

We offer telecom services to activists who are organizing or participating in this event. These services are completely free and open to the public based on the honor system. When you use these services, please be respectful of others and remember it costs us real money.

A conference bridge is a phone number a bunch of people can dial to have a meeting. This is useful if you don't want to reveal your phone number or want to chat with more than three people (because then you could just have a three-way call.)

To get started, ask your group to call into one of the numbers below at a specified time. Please also be aware that anyone is able to listen to your conversation.
