Our Mission

On the 17th of September, AdBusters called for 20,000 people to flood into lower Manhattan, set up tents, kitchens, peaceful barricades and occupy Wall Street for a few months. Once there, we shall incessantly repeat one simple demand in a plurality of voices.

We draw inspiration from the popular assemblies in Egypt's Tahrir Square which have reminded us that real change is possible. We call upon every American who's been betrayed by corruption and greed to join us in this event. Let us follow the example of our Egyptian brothers and systers by peacefully occupying their streets and not going home until our demands have been met.

The time has come to deploy this emerging stratagem against the greatest corrupter of our democracy: Wall Street, the financial Gomorrah of America. It's time for DEMOCRACY NOT CORPORATOCRACY!

This website is meant to serve as a social network for activists wishing to further AdBusters' cause. Through this site you can meet others in your area attending the occupation, offer/receive assistance, and keep up on the latest news.
